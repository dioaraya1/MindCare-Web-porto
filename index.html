How It Works

Initial Consultation

Take the first step towards seeking help by contacting us for an initial consultation. This is an opportunity for us to understand your concerns and provide guidance on the best course of action.

1

2

Assessment

Our experienced psychologists will conduct a comprehensive assessment to identify your specific needs and goals. This may involve interviews, questionnaires, or other evaluation tools.

Customized Therapy Plan

Based on the assessment results, we will create a tailored therapy plan that addresses your unique needs and objectives. This plan will serve as a roadmap for our therapy sessions.

3

4

Therapy Sessions

Regular therapy sessions will be scheduled to work through the customized plan. Our psychologists will employ evidence-based techniques and strategies to support your growth and progress.

Evaluation and Follow-up

Throughout the therapy process, we will continuously monitor your progress and make adjustments to the plan as needed. Regular evaluations and follow-up sessions will ensure that you remain on track towards achieving your goals.

Mental Illness

Mental illness, also known as mental health disorders, refers to conditions that affect thinking, mood, and behavior.

Medication Info

Explore information on mental health medications, which may provide temporary relief from mental illness symptoms.

Therapy Info

Talk therapy is an effective method for addressing mental health issues by transforming distressing emotions, thoughts, and behaviors.

Schizophrenia

Schizophrenia is a severe, long-term, and complex mental health problem that can develop at any point in someone’s life but often begins in adolescence or early adulthood.

Bipolar

Bipolar disorder is a chronic mental illness resulting in unstable moods that cycle between depression (feeling low) and mania (feeling elated). Medical professionals can treat bipolar symptoms with medication and talking therapies.

General questions

What is MindCare Psychology Consulting?

MindCare Psychology Consulting is a professional consulting service that provides expert guidance on mental health and wellness. Our team of licensed psychologists and therapists work with individuals, couples, and organizations to promote emotional well-being and improve relationships.

What services do you offer?

We offer a range of services, including individual therapy, couples therapy, group therapy, workshops, and organizational consulting. Our services are tailored to meet the unique needs of each client.

What is therapy like?

Therapy is a collaborative process between you and your therapist. It's a safe and supportive space to explore your thoughts, feelings, and behaviors. Our therapists use evidence-based techniques to help you achieve your goals.

Do you accept insurance?

Yes, we accept most major insurance providers. However, please check with your insurance provider to confirm coverage.

How do you protect my personal information?

We take the protection of your personal information seriously. We use secure and encrypted systems to store and transmit your data.
